Local Game Harvest

In the years I have been part of Louisville Makes Games, I have met the most talented, skilled, and driven people I know. Louisville Makes Games is a non-profit which aims to promote game creation in Louisville. I serve as the Communication Chair on the board of directors, and I had the honor to promote the biggest event in our ten year history; Local Game Harvest. Local Game Harvest is a yearly exhibition of games created by people in or around Louisville in which the public is invited to try these games and speak with their creators. I reached out to local news outlets and did interviews as the face of the organization on broadcasts from WHAS, Spectrum, and MetroTV. Local Game Harvest was a celebration of those driven and talented people who make video games just because its what they love to do. My job was to put players in front of those games, and I'm proud to say that with more than double the turnout of 2023, we succeeded.

DRE Rebrand

DRE faces a unique challenge in 2025; For most of the company's life, they were known by that name, until 2019 when they were bought and rebranded to Avante Medical Surgical. In 2024, they were bought once again and removed from the Avante umbrella, and are set to do business as DRE once again. Now, they must let their customers know that they are both still here, and returning to form. I designed an updated logo which closely resembles the original so as to be recognizable, but serves as a distinctly modern take. The banner below tells the story of the brand visually, and is being used in email campaigns to get customers up to speed and buying from DRE once again.
